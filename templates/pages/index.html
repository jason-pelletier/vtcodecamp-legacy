Sponsor

Be a Sponsor

The fifth annual Vermont Code Camp will be held in September, 2013 in Burlington, Vermont. This full day event will bring together technology community members, students and professionals from around Vermont and beyond. A wide variety of software technologies will be represented including .NET, PHP, Ruby, Python, Java and more. There is no cost for participants to attend.

Vermont Code Camp is organized entirely by community volunteers, with the help of several sponsors. There are a variety of sponsorship opportunities available, including:

  • Feed us:
    sponsor breakfast, lunch or afternoon snacks
  • Educate us:
    send books, mags, training to raffle
  • Simplify our lives:
    send your great software to raffle
  • Promote us:
    give us advertising space

Contact us to learn more or to become a sponsor: sponsor@vtcodecamp.org
