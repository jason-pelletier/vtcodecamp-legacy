About the Event

Vermont Code Camp is a full day event that brings together technology community members, students and professionals from around Vermont and beyond. In 2015, there were sessions on a variety of software topics including programming, databases, web, business and more.

Check back in the summer of 2016 or follow @VTCodeCamp for information about the next Vermont Code Camp.

Speak

Propose a Session

Vermont Code Camp 2016 will need you! Please consider submitting a speaker session proposal. You don't have to be from Vermont to attend or speak.

Possible topics include .NET, PHP, Ruby, Python, Java…really, anything related to development. You can refer to the 2015 schedule for session ideas.

Sessions are typically 30 minutes to 1 hour in duration, including time for questions. Feel free to submit one or several session proposals.

The deadline for submitting speaker session proposals is typically in August, so be sure to check back before then.

Be a Sponsor

The seventh annual Vermont Code Camp was held in September, 2015 in Burlington, Vermont. This full day event brought together technology community members, students and professionals from around Vermont and beyond. A wide variety of software technologies were represented, and session topics included programming, databases, web, business and more.

Vermont Code Camp is organized entirely by community volunteers, with the help of several sponsors. Sponsorship opportunities typically include:

  • Feed us: sponsor breakfast, lunch or afternoon snacks
  • Educate us: send books, mags, training to raffle
  • Simplify our lives: send your great software to raffle
  • Promote us: give us advertising space

Sponsorship Levels

  • Premiere: $1,000+ or significant in-kind donation
  • Platinum: $750+
  • Gold: $500+
  • Silver: $250+
  • Bronze: $100+
  • Swag: Give-away items (e.g. books, bags, items to put in attendee bags, software licenses, other raffle items, etc.)

Contact us to learn more or to become a sponsor: sponsor@vtcodecamp.org
